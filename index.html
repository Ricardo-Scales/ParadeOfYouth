Our Mission

Parade of Youth is a 501(c)(3) fiscal sponsorship organization dedicated to empowering artists and youth leaders by removing financial and administrative barriers to their work.

We believe in the power of creative expression and youth leadership to transform communities. Our sponsorship model provides the infrastructure and support needed for passionate individuals and organizations to focus on what they do best ‚Äì creating positive change.

Named in honor of Ricardo Scales and his mother Patience Scales, we carry forward a legacy of excellence, dedication, and opening doors for the next generation.
