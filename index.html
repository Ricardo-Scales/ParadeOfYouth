Fiscal Sponsorship FAQ

What is your administrative fee?

Our standard rate is 10% on all contributed income (grants, donations). Government grants may incur a 15% fee due to additional reporting requirements. This covers all administrative services including bookkeeping, tax filings, insurance, and compliance support.

Do I lose ownership of my work?

Absolutely not. Parade of Youth practices Model C fiscal sponsorship, meaning you retain full ownership of your intellectual property and creative assets. Your work remains yourswe simply provide the nonprofit infrastructure.

How long does the application process take?

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Once you submit your inquiry and full application, approval typically takes 2-3 weeks. We schedule a consultation call to ensure alignment before finalizing any agreement.

Can I apply for grants immediately?

Yes! Once your fiscal sponsorship agreement is signed, you can immediately begin applying for grants using our 501(c)(3) status and EIN. We'll provide you with all necessary documentation for grant applications.

What types of projects do you accept?

We welcome projects across arts and culture, youth development, cultural preservation, and social justice initiatives. Projects must align with our 501(c)(3) charitable mission and demonstrate clear community benefit. We especially support legacy artists, emerging youth leaders, and cultural preservation initiatives.
